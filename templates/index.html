Stages of Diabetic Retinopathy

No Diabetic Retinopathy

No abnormalities detected in the retinal image.

Mild Nonproliferative Retinopathy

Microaneurysms only - small areas of balloon-like swelling in the retina's blood vessels.

Moderate Nonproliferative Retinopathy

More extensive abnormalities including bleeding in the retina.

Severe Nonproliferative Retinopathy

More blood vessels are blocked, depriving areas of the retina of blood supply.

Proliferative Retinopathy

Most advanced stage - new blood vessels grow, which are fragile and can leak blood.
